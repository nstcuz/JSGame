Goomb Card Game

Rules

The player starts the game by clicking on two cards from the grid to reveal them.

If the two cards match, they will remain face up, and those cards are considered "matched." If the two cards do not match, they will flip back over, and the player can try again.

The player continues flipping over two cards at a time, trying to remember the position and identity of previously flipped cards.

The game continues until all pairs of cards have been matched and removed from the grid.
